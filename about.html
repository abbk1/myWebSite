ABOUT US

COMPANY PROFILE

Yaysib Computers is a limited liability company, incorporated under the Laws of Corporate Affairs Commission, to provide IT training, products and services to the local corporate market and home users. Yaysib Computer began its operations in 1996 as a training institute, provider of computer accessories to home users and small businesses. Today, Yaysib Computers is a full service computer company servicing home users, and a wide spectrum of business organizations including governmental and non-governmental agencies. Yaysib Computer is a result oriented IT consultancy firm. We intend to fill the market’s need for a professional and customer focused Technology company. The company emphasizes on customer’s service and support to differentiate itself from more price-oriented computer companies. We have also developed a whole new level of expertise towards the current trend in technology which has carved a niche out for the company. More so, it gives us a competitive edge over other existing companies. Yaysib Computer is a leading ICT services provider based in Maiduguri, Borno State of Nigerian, privately owned and operated by a team of expert’s head by Musa Yerima. The company intends to achieve a flexible and responsive service system through solution approach that is customer centric and in which our customers’ business objectives enjoys top priority. By continuing to provide the very best professional service at affordable rates, we have built a large client base spanning from home users, small to medium sized businesses and professional practitioners, through to large corporations and government institutions.
